Smart Sensing Instruments’ Raman spectrometers focusing on following markets:

In 2016, the total Raman Spectrometer market share is around $1.7bn. It has annual growth of 10%.

Raw Material Processing: pharma industries, chemical plant, R&D labs, more and more applications require prescreening for the incoming material, or first article inspection. Raman spectrometer provides a powerful tool to do fast and accurate raw material screening. It saves time and money.

Process Control: industrial customers use Raman spectrometer in the area of process control to improve manufacturing quality and yield. The 785nm laser can efficiently eliminate background fluorescence. Fast detection speeds up the production and testing. The flexibility of handheld Raman spectrometer makes it as a mobile lab grade test station. The Ruggedness of handheld Raman spectrometer make it suitable for harsh environmental use.

Security: in metropolitan cities, people travels with expanding public transportation systems. The severity of safety must be monitored everywhere. Raman spectrometer can identify unknown molecules, leads to identify flammable liquid, solid, hazardous stuff, even drugs. The point-shoot-search/found of Raman spectrometer can finished even in seconds which makes the public more safe and meanwhile alleviate the law-enforcing officials pressure.

Personal Health Monitoring: in the long run, with the personal health market’s emerging, Raman spectrometer will enter every home. It can help you to control the allergic sources; comply with the restrictions of daily consumption from your doctor’s suggestions; identify unhealthy food, etc.
